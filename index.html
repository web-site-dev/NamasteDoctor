About NamasteDoctor

This is an initiative started by doctors in Nepal to promote patient health advocacy by educating people on sexual health topics that are often left unspoken. Many of us feel too shy or hesitant to ask questions about sexual and reproductive health. As a result, we sometimes end up following unhealthy practices without even realizing it. But sexuality is a basic part of life. The more we treat it as a taboo, the more vicious the cycle of misinformation and poor sexual health becomes.
